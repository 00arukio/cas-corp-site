01 — About

A studio built

/ ABOUT.01

We build anime-inspired worlds with deep combat, systems you can sink hundreds of hours into, and the kind of visual polish most people don't expect on Roblox.

Founded in 2023, Cas Corp is a remote-first studio of designers, programmers, animators, and artists from across the Roblox ecosystem. We ship small, iterate fast, and treat our community like collaborators.

Our focus: make every second of player time feel intentional.

Help & contact.

Common questions and where to reach us if you run into problems.

How do I report a bug?
+
Join our Discord and post in the #bug-reports channel. Include your username, what you were doing, and a screenshot or video if you have one.
I lost items / progress — can you restore them?
+
We review cases on a per-incident basis. Open a ticket in Discord with your username, approximate time the issue happened, and a description of what was lost. We can't restore items lost through normal gameplay or trades.
How do I redeem a code?
+
In-game, open the menu and select Codes. Paste the code exactly as shown — codes are case-sensitive. New codes are posted on the Codes page and our socials.
Is there a way to support the game?
+
Yes — like and favorite the game on Roblox, share it with friends, and join our community server. In-game Robux purchases directly support development.
How do I contact the team?
+
Discord is the fastest. For business or press inquiries, DM us on Twitter/X.

Still need help?

Our community team and developers are most active on Discord. You'll usually get a response within 24 hours.
